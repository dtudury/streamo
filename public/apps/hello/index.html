streamo hello

The smallest possible streamo app — five moves in main.js, top-to-bottom. Enter credentials, write entries, watch them appear. Copy-paste and adapt.

Your credentials derive a deterministic keypair via PBKDF2; same credentials always produce the same identity. Made-up credentials get a made-up identity that's still yours, signed and content-addressed. Open main.js in another tab to read along.
